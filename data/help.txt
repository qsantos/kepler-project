=== HELP ===

Key bindings
============

Navigation
----------

Escape       exit
Left click   switch focus to celestial body
Right click  drag and drop to move the view
Mouse wheel  zoom in and out

Display
-------

F11          toggle fullscreen
w            toggle wireframe mode
o            toggle orbits and other helpers
h            toggle this help

Time
----

, (comma)    slow down
. (period)   speed up
/ (slash)    reset to normal time speed
i            invert the arrow of time
= (equal)    go back to present
